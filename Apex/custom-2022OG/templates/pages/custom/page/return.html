Return Policy

RETURN POLICY & STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF SALE

NO HASSLE RETURNS

If, for any reason, you are not completely satisfied with your Apexfoot.com order, you may return your merchandise within 30 days from the date of purchase.

Please return merchandise in its original box if possible and include the provided return slip with returns instructions. Unfortunately, we cannot refund your original shipping charge.

U.S. CUSTOMERS:

We are experiencing operational delays as a result of Covid-19; please allow extra time for processing of your return. All returns are being processed in the order in which they were received. Once processed, your refund will be issued to the credit card or bank account used for the initial purchase. You should see the refund post to your statement within 1-2 billing cycles.

CANADIAN CUSTOMERS:

We are experiencing operational delays as a result of Covid-19, please allow extra time for processing of your return. All returns are being processed in the order in which they were received Once processed, your refund will be issued to the credit card or bank account used for the initial purchase. You should see the refund post to your statement within 1-2 billing cycles.

Please note that refunds are not offered for merchandise returned after the 30-day period.

Warranty: Apex warrants that, upon delivery, goods will be free from defects in material and workmanship under proper care and normal use. Goods shall be considered “defective” if the defect materially impairs the value of the goods for their intended use to the customer. Apex further warrants that all goods and services will be produced and furnished in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

If you believe your item(s) may be defective, please call 1-800-252-2739 between the hours of 8:30 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. EST, Monday through Friday, for further evaluation and instruction on returning potentially defective merchandise.
