About Apex

Apex Foot Health has been in business since 1946, offering premium products that help people enjoy a life of independence and freedom from pain. After leading the charge in designing and manufacturing orthotic shoe inserts in our early years, we applied our knowledge of foot health to designing and manufacturing therapeutic and diabetic shoes in 1971.

We believe that helping others stay healthy and active is a responsibility that must be taken seriously. That’s why all our footwear, socks, compression hosiery and orthotic inserts are designed and manufactured – from start to finish – in consultation with foot health experts, pedorthists and doctors.
